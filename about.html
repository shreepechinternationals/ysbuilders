About US

Since 2066 B.S. (2009 A.D.)

Headquarter: Baijanath, Nepalgunj, Nepal

Branch: Kupondole, Lalitpur, Nepal

YS Builders has established itself as a key player in Nepal's construction industry, delivering exceptional services for both government and private sector projects. We are well-positioned to meet the construction needs of the western and central regions of Nepal.


Government Projects :

In the government sector, YS Builders specializes in the construction of essential infrastructure, including highways, bridges, public buildings, schools, hospitals, and government offices. Our commitment to these projects enhances national connectivity and public services while aligning with long-term development goals. We navigate the complexities of public tenders and adhere to strict compliance regulations, ensuring that each project meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

Private Projects :

In the private sector, we manage a diverse array of projects, from residential complexes and commercial buildings to industrial facilities. This versatility allows us to respond effectively to market demands and provide tailored construction solutions. Our focus on efficiency, customization, and innovation ensures that we meet the unique needs of every client, from individual homeowners to large corporations.

Commitment to Growth

By engaging in both sectors, YS Builders demonstrates its dedication to fulfilling Nepal's diverse infrastructure needs. Our work significantly contributes to the country's growth and development, reinforcing our role as a trusted partner in building a better future for all.
